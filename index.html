Welcome to the Kota Kemuning 2026 Bible Conference Registration Page. The annual conference will be held at Mi Le Garden Resort, Sekinchan, from 16th – 19th December 2026 (Wednesday to Saturday).

The registration for this conference is unique because Conference Registration and Hotel Registration are two different registrations. Please take time to read the instructions.

1 Conference Registration

All attendees are required to complete the Conference Registration form.

Conference registration includes meals for the duration of the conference:

All meals will be served at the conference venue, Mi Le Garden Resort, Sekinchan.

2 Hotel Registration

We have negotiated with three hotel properties at preferential rates for conference attendees who require lodging throughout the event. Please refer to the Hotel Registration page for full details.

Do note that hotel registration is optional. Attendees may choose to arrange their own accommodation outside of the 3 hotel properties. However, all who attend the conference are required to register for the conference.

Important Priority Rule Hotel bookings will be prioritised for attendees who register for the full conference duration (4 days, 3 nights). Partial conference attendance will only be opened after 30th June. This is to prevent the situation where rooms are taken up only partially, hence the priority is given to those who will occupy the room throughout the whole conference.

3 Payment

The conference fee is RM xxx per night (Children’s rate: ___), inclusive of meals. There is no option for non-meal registration.

  • Payment for the conference is to be made directly to the KK Assembly.
  • International attendees may make payment upon arrival at the conference.
  • Payment for hotel accommodation is to be made directly to the respective hotel. The hotel will contact you after your booking to arrange payment.
